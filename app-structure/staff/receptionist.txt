Receptionist extends Employee
    - Handles walk-in or phone call bookings (Guest account is optional but not needed)
        - If the guest has an account, they can give their email so that booking history will be recorded
    - Search/view bookings
    - Edit/cancel bookings
    - Checkin/Checkout guests
    - Quick guest registration for walk-ins or phone call (Optional if the guest chooses it)
    - Filter to make finding specific rooms easier
    - Live notifications for when the a guest makes a booking via web app
